About Me

Moving to Georgia, graduating with a Bachelors of Art from Savannah College of Art and Design and now I find myself in Montreal, Canada.

Now gaining new skills and experience at a visual effects studio, I'm ready for the next part of my journey!

I look forward to what the future holds!
